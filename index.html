How It Works

DeFi in 3 steps

Start earning MLT rewards in minutes. No complicated setup, no lock-up periods.

Step 01
Connect Wallet
Connect your Rabby or MetaMask wallet. The app auto-switches to LiteForge Testnet (Chain 4441).
Step 02
Stake MLT
Approve and deposit your MLT tokens into the farm. Your stake is always in your control — withdraw anytime.
Step 03
Earn Rewards
MLT rewards accrue every second based on your share of the pool. Claim anytime with one click.
Features

Built for real DeFi

Everything you expect from a production-grade yield farm — on the fastest Litecoin L2.

No Impermanent Loss
Single-asset staking means your deposit value is never diluted by price divergence between pairs.
Fully On-Chain
All logic lives in auditable smart contracts on LiteForge. No admin keys, no off-chain dependencies.
Real-Time Rewards
Rewards accrue every second based on your pool share. No epoch delays, no waiting periods.
Live APY
APY is calculated on-chain in real time. What you see is always the current rate — never a marketing estimate.
Withdraw Anytime
Zero lock-up periods. Unstake your full position or a partial amount at any time with no penalty.
Multi-Pool Ready
The farm contract supports multiple independent pools. New tokens and reward structures can be added by the owner.
